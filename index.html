What's this?

An informal group, dedicated to the promotion and discussion about the Lean Startup methodology among Portuguese people who are interested in entrepreneurship.

When is it?

Every last thursday of the month, at 9h30 PM. If something changes, we'll let you know through our channels.

Where is it?

It will take place in Factory | Business Center & CoWork, at Braga. Venue may change (check the info box).
