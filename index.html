What's coming next?

30 October, 21h30
Diogo Terroso NearInteraction

The 8th edition of the Lean Startup Meetup Minho will be a Lean UX special and will have as special guest speaker, Diogo Terroso, a Portuguese interaction and media designer. Lean UX is an approach to product development which focus less in the traditional outputs/deliverables that make designers accountable, and more on the outcome of the solution itself, creating a more transparent process focused on the customers' problems and completely open to their feedback. Diogo will talk about the basics of this disruptive approach and lay down some simple tactics for anyone willing to embrace Lean UX and start practicing within their organizations, to create products and experiences that actually resonate with customers.
